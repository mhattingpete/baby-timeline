## Breastfeeding Recommendations (Amning)

### Sundhedsstyrelsen Guidelines

> **Danish:** "I Danmark anbefaler Sundhedsstyrelsen, at børn ammes fuldt, til de er omkring 6 måneder."
>
> **English:** "In Denmark, the Danish Health Authority recommends that children be fully breastfed until they are around 6 months."

> **Danish:** "Verdenssundhedsorganisationen (WHO) anbefaler fuldamning i de første 6 måneder af barnets liv, og herefter en gradvis introduktion af fast føde med fortsat delvis amning til mindst 2 år."
>
> **English:** "The World Health Organization (WHO) recommends full breastfeeding for the first 6 months of the child's life, followed by a gradual introduction of solid food with continued partial breastfeeding until at least 2 years."

| Period | Recommendation | Danish |
|--------|----------------|--------|
| 0-6 months | Full breastfeeding | Fuldamning |
| 6-12 months | Partial breastfeeding + solids | Delamning + fast føde |
| 12-24 months | Can continue breastfeeding | Kan fortsætte amning |

## Formula Feeding (Modermælkserstatning)

### Types of Formula

> **Danish:** "Den type modermælkserstatning som hedder nummer 1, er beregnet fra fødslen, til barnet er 6 måneder. Barnet kan sagtens fortsætte med denne type til 1 års alderen, men man kan også skifte over til nummer 2 efter de 6 måneder."
>
> **English:** "The type of formula called number 1 is intended from birth until the child is 6 months. The child can easily continue with this type until 1 year of age, but you can also switch to number 2 after 6 months."

| Type | Age Range | Danish | Notes |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Formula 1 | 0-6 months | 0-6 måneder | Can continue to 12 months |
| Formula 2 | 6-12 months | 6-12 måneder | Optional switch |

> **Danish:** "En baby kan helt og aldeles leve af modermælkserstatning uden at få andet, indtil der kommer en naturlig overgang til fast føde fra de 6 måneder og frem."
>
> **English:** "A baby can completely live on formula without getting anything else, until there is a natural transition to solid food from 6 months onwards."

## Introduction to Solid Foods (Overgang til Fast Føde)

### Timeline

> **Danish:** "Det første halve år kan langt de fleste børn leve fint udelukkende af modermælk eller modermælkserstatning, men når barnet fylder seks måneder, skal det introduceres for mad."
>
> **English:** "For the first half year, most children can live just fine exclusively on breast milk or formula, but when the child turns six months, it should be introduced to food."

> **Danish:** "Ellers får barnet ikke de næringsstoffer, som er nødvendige for en normal vækst og udvikling efter seks måneders alderen."
>
> **English:** "Otherwise, the child does not get the nutrients necessary for normal growth and development after six months of age."

| Age | Feeding Guideline | Danish |
|-----|-------------------|--------|
| 0-4 months | Breast milk or formula only | Kun mælk |
| 4-6 months | Small taste portions allowed (earliest at 4 months) | Små smagsprøver mulige |
| 6 months | **Must** introduce solid foods | **Skal** introduceres for mad |
| 9 months | Can eat modified family food | Kan spise familiemad (tilpasset) |
| 12 months | Transition away from formula | Udfaser erstatning |

> **Danish:** "Fra omkring 9 måneder kan barnet spise det samme mad som resten af familien, fortsat med modermælk eller modermælkserstatning som supplement frem til barnet bliver 1 år."
>
> **English:** "From around 9 months, the child can eat the same food as the rest of the family, still with breast milk or formula as a supplement until the child turns 1 year."

### Important: No Cow's Milk Before 1 Year

> **Danish:** "Anbefalingen er, at børn frem til 1-års alderen ikke må få almindelig drikkemælk, men skal ernæres enten på bryst eller modermælkserstatning – ved siden af en sund og alsidig kost, som er tilpasset spædbarnets alder."
>
> **English:** "The recommendation is that children until 1 year of age should not have regular drinking milk, but should be fed either by breast or formula – alongside a healthy and varied diet adapted to the infant's age."

## Fat and Energy Requirements

> **Danish:** "Indtil barnet er et år, har det brug for ekstra fedtstof i maden for at få energi nok. Tilsæt én teskefuld fedtstof til hjemmelavet grød og mos for at sikre, at barnet vokser som forventet."
>
> **English:** "Until the child is one year old, it needs extra fat in the food to get enough energy. Add one teaspoon of fat to homemade porridge and puree to ensure the child grows as expected."

| Recommendation | Danish |
|----------------|--------|
| Add 1 tsp fat to homemade food | Tilsæt én teskefuld fedtstof |
| Vary fat sources | Skift mellem smør, olier og plantemargarine |

## D-Vitamin Supplementation

### Sundhedsstyrelsen Guidelines (Updated December 2020)

> **Danish:** "Fra barnet er 2 uger gammelt, skal det dagligt have et tilskud af D-vitamin, dette fortsættes indtil barnet fylder 4 år."
>
> **English:** "From when the child is 2 weeks old, it should daily have a supplement of D-vitamin, this continues until the child turns 4 years."

> **Danish:** "Sundhedsstyrelsens dagligt anbefalede dosis er 10 mikrogram (400 IE) D-vitamin om dagen."
>
> **English:** "The Danish Health Authority's daily recommended dose is 10 micrograms (400 IU) of D-vitamin per day."

| Age | Dose | Form | Danish |
|-----|------|------|--------|
| 2 weeks - 4 years | 10 μg (400 IU) daily | Drops (dråber) | 5 dråber dagligt |
| After 1.5 years | 10 μg | Can switch to tablets | Kan skifte til tabletter |

### Exception for Formula-Fed Babies

> **Danish:** "Man anbefaler ikke D-vitamintilskud til spædbørn, som dagligt får mindst 800 ml modermælkserstatning med 1,3 µg D-vitamin pr 100 ml."
>
> **English:** "D-vitamin supplementation is not recommended for infants who daily receive at least 800 ml formula with 1.3 µg D-vitamin per 100 ml."

| Condition | D-Vitamin Supplement Needed? |
|-----------|------------------------------|
| Breastfed | Yes - always |
| Formula < 800 ml/day | Yes |
| Formula ≥ 800 ml/day (with 1.3 μg/100ml) | No |

### Why D-Vitamin is Important

> **Danish:** "Vitamin D er nødvendigt for barnets normale vækst og knogleudvikling og bidrager til immunsystemets normale funktion."
>
> **English:** "Vitamin D is necessary for the child's normal growth and bone development and contributes to the immune system's normal function."

> **Danish:** "Selvom vi danner D-vitamin i huden med hjælp fra solens stråler i sommerhalvåret, er det vigtigt, at små børn får tilskud af D-vitamin i de første fire år, fordi det er en periode, hvor de vokser meget, og hvor knoglerne gøres stærke."
>
> **English:** "Although we produce D-vitamin in the skin with the help of the sun's rays in summer, it is important that small children get D-vitamin supplements in the first four years, because it is a period when they grow a lot, and when bones are made strong."

### After 4 Years

> **Danish:** "Fødevarestyrelsen anbefaler, at alle voksne og børn over 4 år tager et tilskud på mellem 5-10 mikrogram D-vitamin i vinterhalvåret fra oktober til og med april."
>
> **English:** "The Danish Food Administration recommends that all adults and children over 4 years take a supplement of 5-10 micrograms D-vitamin in winter from October through April."

> **Danish:** "Børn med mørk hud skal fortsætte med D-vitamin gennem hele barndommen."
>
> **English:** "Children with dark skin should continue with D-vitamin throughout childhood."

## Allergy Prevention (Allergiforebyggelse)

### Updated Guidelines (Sundhedsstyrelsen 2022)

Recent research has changed allergy prevention guidelines. Early introduction of common allergens may reduce allergy risk.

#### Egg Introduction

> **Danish:** "Forekomsten af allergi over for æg kan mindskes ved fra 4-6 måneders alderen at give barnet et halvt lille hårdkogt æg sammen med anden mad to gange om ugen."
>
> **English:** "The occurrence of egg allergy can be reduced by giving the child half a small hard-boiled egg with other food twice a week from 4-6 months of age."
>
> *Source: Sundhedsstyrelsen 2022*

| Recommendation | Details |
|----------------|---------|
| When to start | 4-6 months of age |
| Amount | Half a small hard-boiled egg |
| Frequency | Twice per week |
| How | Mixed with other food |

#### General Allergy Prevention

| Guideline | Danish | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Varied diet during pregnancy | Alsidig kost under graviditet | Mother's diet may influence baby |
| Varied diet during breastfeeding | Alsidig kost under amning | Exposure through breast milk |
| Early introduction of allergens | Tidlig introduktion af allergener | May reduce allergy risk |
| Don't delay common allergens | Udskyd ikke almindelige allergener | No benefit to waiting past 6 months |

### Common Allergens to Introduce

After 4-6 months, gradually introduce:

| Food | Danish | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Egg | Æg | Hard-boiled, well-cooked |
| Peanut | Jordnød | Peanut butter, not whole nuts |
| Fish | Fisk | Well-cooked |
| Wheat | Hvede | In cereals, bread |
| Milk products | Mælkeprodukter | Yogurt, cheese (not cow's milk as drink) |

**Note:** Introduce one new food at a time, wait 2-3 days before introducing another.

### When to Be Cautious

Consult doctor before introducing allergens if:
- Family history of severe food allergies
- Baby has eczema
- Baby has existing known allergies
- Previous allergic reactions

## Water for Babies (Vand til Spædbørn)

### Guidelines

> **Danish:** "Indtil barnet er et år, skal det som udgangspunkt helst have modermælk/modermælkserstatning, når det tilbydes noget at drikke."
>
> **English:** "Until the child is one year old, it should preferably have breast milk/formula when offered something to drink."
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk*

| Age | Water Recommendation |
|-----|---------------------|
| 0-6 months | Generally not needed (breast milk/formula sufficient) |
| 6-12 months | Small amounts with meals if desired |
| After 12 months | Water as primary drink alongside milk |

### When Water May Be Needed

| Situation | Guidance |
|-----------|----------|
| Hot weather | Small sips between feeds |
| Constipation | May help with solids introduction |
| With solid meals | Small amounts (6+ months) |
| Fever/illness | Offer extra fluids (breast milk/formula preferred) |

### Water Preparation (Under 4 Months)

> **Danish:** "Vand til babyer under 4 måneder skal koges og afkøles først."
>
> **English:** "Water for babies under 4 months should be boiled and cooled first."

| Age | Water Preparation |
|-----|-------------------|
| Under 4 months | Boil and cool tap water |
| After 4 months | Tap water is generally safe |

### What NOT to Give

| Avoid | Danish | Why |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Juice | Saft | Unnecessary sugar, no nutritional benefit |
| Sweetened drinks | Søde drikke | Harmful for teeth and health |
| Cow's milk as drink | Komælk som drik | Not before 1 year |
| Tea/coffee | Te/kaffe | Caffeine, no benefit |
| Bottled water with minerals | Mineralvand | May contain too much sodium |
