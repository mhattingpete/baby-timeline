## Vaccine Details

### DiTeKiPol/Hib (Combined vaccine)

> **Danish:** "Di-Te-Ki-Pol er en kombineret vaccine, der giver beskyttelse mod difteri (som kan give alvorlig hals- og hjertebetændelse), stivkrampe (som kan fås ved sårinfektion), kighoste (som især er alvorlig hos små børn), og polio (som kan medføre blivende lammelser)."
>
> **English:** "Di-Te-Ki-Pol is a combined vaccine that provides protection against diphtheria (which can cause serious throat and heart inflammation), tetanus (which can be contracted through wound infection), pertussis (which is especially serious in young children), and polio (which can lead to permanent paralysis)."

| Disease | Danish | Severity |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Diphtheria | Difteri | Serious throat/heart inflammation |
| Tetanus | Stivkrampe | From wound infection |
| Pertussis | Kighoste | Especially dangerous for infants |
| Polio | Polio | Can cause permanent paralysis |
| Hib | Hib-infektion | Meningitis, epiglottitis |

> **Danish:** "Di-Te-Ki-Pol gives sammen med Pneumokokvaccinen ved barnealderen 3 måneder, 5 måneder og 12 måneder, og ved 5-års-alderen gives Di-Te-Ki-pol (uden Hib)."
>
> **English:** "Di-Te-Ki-Pol is given together with the Pneumococcal vaccine at child ages 3 months, 5 months and 12 months, and at 5 years of age Di-Te-Ki-pol is given (without Hib)."

### MFR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)

> **Danish:** "Den levende svækkede MFR-vaccine gives i alderen 15 måneder og 4 år, i forbindelse med børneundersøgelsen hos egen læge."
>
> **English:** "The live attenuated MFR vaccine is given at ages 15 months and 4 years, in connection with child examinations at the family doctor."

> **Danish:** "MFR består af levende svækket mæslingevirus, parotitisvirus og rubellavirus. Beskyttelsen efter de anbefalede to doser er formentlig livslang."
>
> **English:** "MFR consists of live attenuated measles virus, mumps virus, and rubella virus. Protection after the recommended two doses is probably lifelong."

| Disease | Danish | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Measles | Mæslinger | Highly contagious |
| Mumps | Fåresyge | Can cause hearing loss |
| Rubella | Røde hunde | Dangerous during pregnancy |

### Pneumococcal Vaccine (Pneumokokvaccine)

Protects against infections caused by pneumococcal bacteria:
- Meningitis
- Blood poisoning (sepsis)
- Pneumonia
- Ear infections

## Protection Duration

> **Danish:** "Efter revaccination anses beskyttelsen mod difteri, tetanus og kighoste at vare 10 år."
>
> **English:** "After revaccination, protection against diphtheria, tetanus and pertussis is considered to last 10 years."

| Vaccine | Duration |
|---------|----------|
| DiTeKiPol | ~10 years after booster |
| MFR | Probably lifelong after 2 doses |

---

## Coverage in Denmark (2024)

> **Danish:** "Danmark har en stabil og høj tilslutning til børnevaccinationsprogrammet. For flere vacciner lever Danmark allerede op til WHO's målsætninger for 2030."
>
> **English:** "Denmark has stable and high adherence to the childhood vaccination program. For several vaccines, Denmark already meets WHO's 2030 targets."

> **Danish:** "I 2024 ses over 95 % tilslutning til vaccination mod difteri, stivkrampe, kighoste, polio, hib-infektion og pneumokokvaccination, der tilbydes i barnets første leveår."
>
> **English:** "In 2024, over 95% adherence is seen for vaccination against diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, polio, hib infection and pneumococcal vaccination offered in the child's first year of life."

---

## Pregnancy Vaccination (2024)

> **Danish:** "Fra 2024 blev der i Danmark startet et tilbud til alle gravide om kighostevaccination i form af di-te-ki-vaccination i sidste del af graviditeten, for at beskytte nyfødte og spædbørn i deres allerførste levetid."
>
> **English:** "From 2024, an offer was started in Denmark for all pregnant women for pertussis vaccination in the form of di-te-ki vaccination in the last part of pregnancy, to protect newborns and infants in their very first life."
