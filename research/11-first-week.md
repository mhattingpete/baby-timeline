## Key Quote

> **Danish:** "I dit barns første leveuge kan du let tjekke, om det får nok at spise ved at holde øje med bleerne."
>
> **English:** "In your baby's first week, you can easily check if they're getting enough to eat by monitoring the diapers."
>
> *Source: ammenet.dk*

## Day-by-Day Expectations

The first week is crucial for establishing feeding and monitoring that baby is getting adequate nutrition. Track wet diapers, dirty diapers, and feeding frequency daily.

### Day 1 (Dag 1)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 1-2 | 1-2 våde bleer | Urine may be pink/orange (urate crystals) - normal |
| Dirty diapers | 1-2 | 1-2 afføringsbleer | Meconium (black, tar-like) |
| Feedings | 4-5 minimum | Mindst 4-5 måltider | Colostrum is small in volume but nutrient-dense |
| Poop appearance | Black, sticky, tar-like | Sort, klæbrig, tjæreagtig | Meconium (mekonium) |

**Notes:** Baby may be sleepy. Skin-to-skin contact is very important. Wake baby to feed if necessary.

### Day 2 (Dag 2)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 2-3 | 2-3 våde bleer | |
| Dirty diapers | 1-2 | 1-2 afføringsbleer | Still meconium or transitional |
| Feedings | 6-8 | 6-8 måltider | |
| Poop appearance | Black to dark green | Sort til mørkegrøn | Transitional stool beginning |

### Day 3 (Dag 3)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 3-4 | 3-4 våde bleer | Urine should be clearer |
| Dirty diapers | 2-3 | 2-3 afføringsbleer | Transitional stool |
| Feedings | 8-12 | 8-12 måltider | Milk coming in (breast fullness) |
| Poop appearance | Dark green to brown | Mørkegrøn til brun | Transitional (overgangsafføring) |

**Notes:** Milk typically "comes in" on days 2-4. Baby may be more wakeful and hungry.

### Day 4 (Dag 4)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 4-5 | 4-5 våde bleer | |
| Dirty diapers | 3-4 | 3-4 afføringsbleer | |
| Feedings | 8-12 | 8-12 måltider | |
| Poop appearance | Brown to yellow-green | Brun til gulgrøn | Becoming lighter |

### Day 5 (Dag 5)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 5-6 | 5-6 våde bleer | |
| Dirty diapers | 3-4 | 3-4 afføringsbleer | |
| Feedings | 8-12 | 8-12 måltider | |
| Poop appearance | Yellow-green to yellow | Gulgrøn til gul | Stool should be getting lighter |

### Day 6 (Dag 6)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 6+ | 6+ våde bleer | Target reached |
| Dirty diapers | 3-4 | 3-4 afføringsbleer | |
| Feedings | 8-12 | 8-12 måltider | |
| Poop appearance | Yellow | Gul | Should be yellow by now |

### Day 7 (Dag 7)

| Indicator | Expected | Danish | Notes |
|-----------|----------|--------|-------|
| Wet diapers | 6+ | 6+ våde bleer | Stable at 6+ per day |
| Dirty diapers | 3-4 | 3-4 afføringsbleer | |
| Feedings | 8-12 | 8-12 måltider | Established pattern |
| Poop appearance | Yellow, seedy | Gul, grynlig | Normal breastfed stool |

## Weight Changes (Vægtændringer)

### Normal Weight Loss

> **Danish:** "Det er normalt, at barnet taber sig de første dage efter fødslen."
>
> **English:** "It is normal for the baby to lose weight in the first days after birth."
>
> *Source: ammenet.dk*

| Timeframe | Expected | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Days 1-3 | Weight loss up to 7% | Normal physiological weight loss |
| Day 3-4 | Lowest weight point | Weight should stabilize |
| By day 10-14 | Birth weight regained | Should return to birth weight |
| After 2 weeks | Steady weight gain | 150-250g per week expected |

### Warning Signs for Weight

| Concern | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Weight loss >10% | Contact midwife/doctor |
| Not regained birth weight by 2 weeks | Contact healthcare provider |
| No weight gain by week 3 | Seek feeding evaluation |

## Feeding Frequency (Ammefrekvens)

### Breastfed Babies

| Period | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| First 24 hours | Minimum 4-5 feeds | Baby may be sleepy, wake to feed |
| Days 2-7 | 8-12 feeds per day | Every 2-3 hours, including at night |
| After first week | 8-12 feeds per day | Feed on demand |

### Signs of Adequate Feeding

> **Danish:** "Tegn på at barnet får nok at spise."
>
> **English:** "Signs that the baby is getting enough to eat."

| Sign | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Wet diapers | 6+ per day after day 5 |
| Dirty diapers | 3-4 per day (breastfed) |
| Audible swallowing | Can hear baby swallow during feeds |
| Satisfied after feeds | Relaxed, releases breast naturally |
| Weight gain | Gaining after initial loss |

## Stool Transition (Afføringsovergang)

### Sundhedsstyrelsen "Tjek Bleen" Guidelines

| Day | Expected Color | Danish | Type |
|-----|----------------|--------|------|
| Day 1-2 | Black | Sort | Meconium (mekonium) |
| Day 3-4 | Dark green/brown | Mørkegrøn/brun | Transitional (overgangsafføring) |
| Day 5+ | Yellow-green | Gulgrøn | Transitional to normal |
| Day 7+ | Yellow, seedy | Gul, grynlig | Normal breastfed stool |

**Important:** Stool should be yellow by day 7. If still green/dark after day 7, contact healthcare provider.

---

## Meconium (Mekonium)

| Characteristic | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| Color | Black/dark green |
| Consistency | Sticky, tar-like |
| Timing | First stool within 24-48 hours |
| Duration | Passed completely by day 3-4 |

> **Danish:** "Mekonium er den første afføring, barnet har. Den er sort og klæbrig."
>
> **English:** "Meconium is the baby's first stool. It is black and sticky."

## When to Contact Healthcare Provider (Hvornår kontakte sundhedspersonale)

### Urgent - Contact Immediately

| Sign | Danish | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| No meconium within 48 hours | Ingen mekonium inden for 48 timer | Contact immediately |
| No wet diapers for 12+ hours | Ingen våde bleer i 12+ timer | Contact immediately |
| Baby very lethargic, won't wake to feed | Baby meget sløv, vil ikke vågne til amning | Contact immediately |
| Fever (>38°C) | Feber (>38°C) | Contact immediately |

### Concerning - Contact Within 24 Hours

| Sign | Danish | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| Fewer wet diapers than expected | Færre våde bleer end forventet | Contact within 24 hours |
| Stool still black/green after day 5 | Afføring stadig sort/grøn efter dag 5 | Contact within 24 hours |
| Weight loss >7% | Vægttab >7% | Contact within 24 hours |
| Persistent feeding difficulties | Vedvarende ammevanskeligheder | Contact within 24 hours |

## Additional Notes

### Pink/Orange Urine (Urate Crystals)

In the first few days, you may see pink or orange spots in the diaper. These are **urate crystals** and are normal in the first 2-3 days. If they persist beyond day 3, it may indicate baby is not getting enough milk.

> **Danish:** "Lyserøde eller orange pletter i bleen de første dage skyldes uratkrystaller og er normalt."
>
> **English:** "Pink or orange spots in the diaper in the first days are due to urate crystals and are normal."

### Cluster Feeding

Babies often cluster feed (feed very frequently) especially in the evenings during the first weeks. This is normal and helps establish milk supply.

> **Danish:** "Klyngeamning er normalt og hjælper med at etablere mælkeproduktionen."
>
> **English:** "Cluster feeding is normal and helps establish milk production."
