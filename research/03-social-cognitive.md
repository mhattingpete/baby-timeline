## Key Social Development Concepts

### Attachment (Tilknytning)
The bond between baby and caregivers forms the foundation for social development. Signs of secure attachment include:
- Seeking comfort from caregivers when distressed
- Using caregiver as a "safe base" for exploration
- Showing preference for familiar people

### Stranger Anxiety (Fremmedangst)
> Typically begins around 8 months when babies learn to distinguish familiar faces from strangers. This is a **normal and healthy** developmental sign.

### Object Permanence (Objektpermanens)
> Around 8-12 months, babies understand that objects continue to exist even when out of sight. This is why they start searching for hidden toys.
