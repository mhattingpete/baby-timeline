# Baby Development Research - Denmark

This directory contains comprehensive research documentation for building a baby development tracking website, with data specific to **Denmark** and sourced from official Danish health authorities.

---

## How to Use This Documentation

1. **Review each file** - Check that the data matches the cited sources
2. **Verify Danish quotes** - Ensure translations are accurate
3. **Check the verification checklists** - Each file has a checklist at the bottom
4. **Flag any issues** - Note any data that seems incorrect

Once you've verified the data, implementation can proceed.

## Quick Reference: Key Numbers

### Sleep (Sundhedsstyrelsen)
- **0-3 months**: 14-17 hours (acceptable: 11-19)
- **4-11 months**: 12-15 hours (acceptable: 10-18)
- **1-2 years**: 11-14 hours

### Outdoor Sleep
- **Temperature limit**: -10°C
- **Minimum weight**: 3000g

### D-Vitamin
- **Dose**: 10 μg (400 IU) daily
- **Start**: 2 weeks old
- **End**: 4 years old

### Key Milestones
- **Sits**: 6-9 months
- **Walks**: 12-18 months
- **First words**: 9-12 months
- **Two-word sentences**: ~24 months

### Børneundersøgelser
7 examinations: 5 weeks, 5 months, 12 months, 2 years, 3 years, 4 years, 5 years

### Vaccinations
- **3, 5, 12 months**: DiTeKiPol/Hib + Pneumococcal
- **15 months**: MFR (1st)
- **4 years**: MFR (2nd)
- **5 years**: DiTeKiPol booster

### First Week (NEW)
- **Wet diapers**: 6+ per day by day 5-6
- **Feedings**: 8-12 per day
- **Weight loss**: Normal up to 7%, regain by 2 weeks
- **Stool**: Should be yellow by day 7

### Emergency Thresholds (NEW)
- **Under 6 months + fever >38°C**: See doctor immediately
- **SIDS prevention**: Always back to sleep, own bed, same room
- **Jaundice**: 60% affected, resolves in 1-2 weeks, concern if >14 days

### Sun Protection (NEW)
- **Non-mobile infants**: Keep out of direct sunlight entirely
- **UV protection needed**: When UV index ≥3 (April-September)
