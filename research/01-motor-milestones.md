## Important Note on Milestones

> **Danish:** "Alle børn tilegner sig mentale og motoriske færdigheder i samme rækkefølge"
>
> **English:** "All children acquire mental and motor skills in the same sequence"
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk - Børns udvikling, milepæle*

Children develop at different rates, but follow the same sequence. The ages below are guidelines, not strict deadlines.

## When to Contact a Doctor

> **Danish:** "Et barn, der ikke kan gå ved 18 måneders alderen, bør vurderes af en børnelæge."
>
> **English:** "A child who cannot walk by 18 months of age should be evaluated by a pediatrician."
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk - Børns udvikling, milepæle*

### Red flags to discuss with sundhedsplejerske or læge:
- Significant delay in reaching milestones compared to ranges above
- Loss of previously acquired skills
- Asymmetrical movement (using one side more than the other)
- Persistent stiffness or floppiness
