## Overview

Colic is excessive, unexplained crying in an otherwise healthy baby. It is common, affecting 8-40% of infants, and while distressing for parents, it is not dangerous and resolves on its own.

> **Danish:** "Spædbarnskolik er en tilstand hos spædbørn, hvor barnet græder mere end normalt, uden at der kan findes en årsag."
>
> **English:** "Infant colic is a condition in infants where the baby cries more than normal, without a cause being found."
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk*

---

## Definition (The Rule of 3s)

| Criteria | Danish | Details |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Crying >3 hours/day | Gråd >3 timer/dag | Intense, inconsolable crying |
| >3 days/week | >3 dage/uge | Pattern over time |
| In baby <5 months | Hos barn <5 måneder | Age limit |
| Otherwise healthy | Ellers sundt barn | No underlying illness |

## Timeline

### When Does It Start?

> **Danish:** "Spædbarnskolik starter normalt omkring 2. leveuge."
>
> **English:** "Infant colic usually starts around the 2nd week of life."

| Stage | Age | Description |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| Onset | ~2 weeks | Symptoms typically begin |
| Peak | ~6 weeks | Crying usually worst |
| Gradual improvement | 8-12 weeks | Symptoms start to decrease |
| Resolution | 3-4 months | Usually stops abruptly |
| Maximum duration | 5 months | Always resolved by this age |

### Key Quote on Resolution

> **Danish:** "Spædbarnskolik holder normalt pludseligt op hos de fleste ved 3-4 måneders alder, og hos alle inden barnet er 5 måneder gammel."
>
> **English:** "Infant colic usually stops abruptly in most babies at 3-4 months of age, and in all babies before they are 5 months old."
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk*

## What Causes Colic?

The exact cause is unknown. Possible contributing factors include:

| Possible Factor | Danish | Notes |
|-----------------|--------|-------|
| Immature digestive system | Umodent fordøjelsessystem | May cause discomfort |
| Gas/air swallowing | Gas/synke luft | During feeding or crying |
| Overstimulation | Overstimulering | Sensory overload |
| Normal developmental phase | Normal udviklingsfase | Baby adjusting to world |

> **Danish:** "Årsagen til spædbarnskolik er ukendt."
>
> **English:** "The cause of infant colic is unknown."

**Important:** Colic is NOT caused by:
- Bad parenting
- Something the mother ate (in most cases)
- Spoiling the baby
- Hunger (if feeding normally)

## The 5 S's Method

A popular technique developed by Dr. Harvey Karp:

| S | English | Danish | Description |
|---|---------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Swaddling | Svøbning | Wrap snugly |
| 2 | Side/Stomach | Side/Mave | Hold in this position (not for sleep) |
| 3 | Shushing | Sussen | White noise, "shh" |
| 4 | Swinging | Svingende | Gentle rhythmic motion |
| 5 | Sucking | Suttende | Pacifier or finger |

| Intervention | Danish | Evidence |
|--------------|--------|----------|

| Simethicone drops | Simeticon dråber | Limited evidence |

## When to See a Doctor (Hvornår kontakte læge)

While colic itself is not dangerous, see a doctor to:

### Rule Out Other Causes

| Sign | Danish | Possible Concern |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Fever | Feber | Infection |
| Vomiting | Opkastning | Reflux or illness |
| Diarrhea | Diarré | Infection or intolerance |
| Blood in stool | Blod i afføring | Allergy or other |
| Poor weight gain | Dårlig vægtøgning | Feeding issues |
| No relief, ever | Ingen lettelse, nogensinde | Other diagnosis |

### Red Flags - Seek Help If:

| Warning Sign | Danish |
|--------------|--------|
| Crying changes suddenly | Gråden ændrer sig pludseligt |
| Baby seems in pain | Baby virker smertepåvirket |
| Refuses to eat | Nægter at spise |
| Fever | Feber |
| Bulging fontanelle | Udbulet fontanelle |
| Rash | Udslæt |
| You are worried about your own coping | Du er bekymret for din egen mestring |

## Parent Coping Strategies (Forældres Mestringsstrategier)

### It's Normal to Feel:

| Feeling | Danish | Reassurance |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Frustrated | Frustreret | This is extremely common |
| Exhausted | Udmattet | Sleep deprivation is real |
| Helpless | Hjælpeløs | You're not doing anything wrong |
| Anxious | Angst | Will get better |

### Coping Tips

| Strategy | Danish | Details |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Take turns | Skift ture | Share with partner/family |
| Take breaks | Tag pauser | Put baby safely down, step away |
| Accept help | Acceptér hjælp | Let others hold baby |
| Sleep when baby sleeps | Sov når baby sover | Prioritize rest |
| Talk about it | Tal om det | Share with others going through it |
| Remember it ends | Husk det ender | Maximum 5 months |

### When to Put Baby Down

If you feel overwhelmed:

1. **Put baby in safe place** (crib, on back)
2. **Step away for a few minutes**
3. **Take deep breaths**
4. **Call for help if needed**

> **Danish:** "Det er okay at lægge barnet sikkert ned og gå væk et øjeblik, hvis du føler dig overvældet."
>
> **English:** "It's okay to put the baby down safely and step away for a moment if you feel overwhelmed."

**Never shake a baby.** If you feel at risk of this, put baby down and call for help.

## Key Messages for Parents

1. **You're not alone** - 8-40% of babies have colic
2. **You're not doing anything wrong** - Colic is not your fault
3. **It will end** - Always resolves by 5 months, usually by 3-4 months
4. **It's okay to take a break** - Put baby safely down, step away
5. **Ask for help** - Accept support from family, friends, healthcare
6. **Trust your instincts** - If something seems wrong, see a doctor
7. **Never shake a baby** - If overwhelmed, put baby down and get help
