## Overview

SIDS (Sudden Infant Death Syndrome) is the sudden and unexplained death of an apparently healthy baby under 1 year of age.

> **Danish:** "Vuggedød er den pludselige og uforklarlige død hos et tilsyneladende sundt barn under 1 år."
>
> **English:** "SIDS is the sudden and unexplained death of an apparently healthy baby under 1 year of age."

### Impact of Prevention Guidelines

> **Danish:** "Dødsfaldet faldt fra ca. 120 om året til nu ca. 5 om året efter man begyndte at følge forebyggelsesrådene."
>
> **English:** "Deaths dropped from about 120 per year to now about 5 per year after prevention guidelines were implemented."
>
> *Source: Sundhedsstyrelsen*

This dramatic reduction demonstrates the effectiveness of following safe sleep guidelines.

## The Three Main Risk Factors (De Tre Hovedrisikofaktorer)

### 1. Sleep Position (Sovstilling)

| Risk | Danish | Safe Practice |
|------|--------|---------------|
| Stomach sleeping | Mave | **Always place on back** |
| Side sleeping | Side | Can roll to stomach |
| Back sleeping | Ryg | **SAFEST position** |

> **Danish:** "Læg altid dit barn til at sove på ryggen."
>
> **English:** "Always put your baby to sleep on their back."

### 2. Tobacco Smoke Exposure (Udsættelse for Tobaksrøg)

| Exposure | Risk Increase | Danish |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| Smoking during pregnancy | Significant increase | Rygning under graviditet |
| Secondhand smoke | Significant increase | Passiv rygning |
| Nicotine products | Increased risk | Nikotinprodukter |

> **Danish:** "Undgå tobaksrøg og nikotinprodukter omkring barnet."
>
> **English:** "Avoid tobacco smoke and nicotine products around the baby."

### 3. Overheating (Overophedning)

| Risk Factor | Danish | Prevention |
|-------------|--------|------------|
| Too many blankets | For mange tæpper | Use sleep sack |
| Too warm room | For varmt værelse | Keep 16-20°C |
| Overdressing | For meget tøj | Dress appropriately |
| Covering head | Dækket hoved | Keep head uncovered |

> **Danish:** "Barnet må ikke blive for varmt. Tjek at barnet ikke sveder i nakken."
>
> **English:** "The baby must not get too hot. Check that the baby is not sweating at the neck."

## Safe Sleep Guidelines (Sikker Søvn Retningslinjer)

### The ABCs of Safe Sleep

| Letter | English | Danish | Description |
|--------|---------|--------|-------------|
| A | Alone | Alene | Baby sleeps alone in their own bed |
| B | Back | Ryg | Always on their back |
| C | Crib | Seng | In a safe sleep space |

### Sleep Environment

| Guideline | Danish | Details |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Own sleep space | Eget sovested | Crib, bassinet, or Moses basket |
| Firm mattress | Fast madras | No soft surfaces |
| No loose bedding | Ingen løst sengetøj | No pillows, blankets, toys |
| Empty crib | Tom seng | Only fitted sheet |
| Room temperature | Stuetemperatur | 16-20°C ideal |
| Same room as parents | Samme rum som forældre | For first 6 months minimum |

> **Danish:** "Barnet skal sove i sin egen seng i forældrenes soveværelse de første 6 måneder."
>
> **English:** "The baby should sleep in their own bed in the parents' bedroom for the first 6 months."

## Room Sharing vs. Bed Sharing

### Room Sharing - RECOMMENDED

| Benefit | Danish |
|---------|--------|
| Reduces SIDS risk by ~50% | Reducerer risiko for vuggedød med ~50% |
| Easier nighttime feeding | Nemmere natamning |
| Parents can monitor baby | Forældre kan overvåge baby |

### Bed Sharing - NOT RECOMMENDED

| Risk Factor | Danish | Concern |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| Adult bedding | Voksensengetøj | Suffocation risk |
| Soft mattress | Blød madras | Suffocation risk |
| Pillows | Puder | Suffocation risk |
| Risk of overlay | Risiko for at ligge på barnet | Especially if tired/intoxicated |
| Gaps | Spalter | Entrapment risk |

> **Danish:** "Læg ikke barnet til at sove i jeres seng. Der er risiko for, at barnet kan blive kvalt."
>
> **English:** "Do not put the baby to sleep in your bed. There is a risk that the baby could suffocate."

### When Bed Sharing is Especially Dangerous

| Situation | Danish |
|-----------|--------|
| If parent has consumed alcohol | Hvis forælder har drukket alkohol |
| If parent has taken sedatives | Hvis forælder har taget sovemedicin |
| If parent is extremely tired | Hvis forælder er meget træt |
| If parent smokes | Hvis forælder ryger |
| On soft surfaces (sofa, chair) | På bløde overflader (sofa, stol) |

## Age-Specific Recommendations

### 0-6 Months (Highest Risk Period)

| Guideline | Danish |
|-----------|--------|
| Always back to sleep | Altid på ryggen |
| Room share (same room, separate bed) | Del rum (samme rum, separat seng) |
| No soft objects in sleep area | Ingen bløde genstande i soveområdet |
| Offer pacifier at sleep time | Tilbyd sut ved sovetid |
| Breastfeed if possible | Am hvis muligt |

### 6-12 Months

| Guideline | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Continue back to sleep | Until baby can roll independently |
| Continue room sharing | Recommended until 12 months |
| Once baby can roll both ways | Allow to find comfortable position |

### After 12 Months

SIDS risk decreases significantly after 12 months, but safe sleep practices should continue.

## Protective Factors (Beskyttende Faktorer)

| Factor | Danish | Effect |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Breastfeeding | Amning | Reduces risk |
| Pacifier use | Sutbrug | Reduces risk |
| Room sharing | Deling af værelse | Reduces risk ~50% |
| Immunizations | Vaccinationer | Reduces risk |
| Prenatal care | Svangerskabskontrol | Reduces risk |

> **Danish:** "Amning beskytter mod vuggedød."
>
> **English:** "Breastfeeding protects against SIDS."

## What NOT to Do (Hvad man IKKE skal gøre)

| Don't | Danish | Why |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Use soft bedding | Brug blød sengetøj | Suffocation risk |
| Use pillows | Brug puder | Suffocation risk |
| Use bumper pads | Brug sengehest puder | Suffocation risk |
| Leave toys in crib | Efterlad legetøj i sengen | Suffocation risk |
| Cover baby's head | Dæk babyens hoved | Overheating risk |
| Let baby overheat | Lad baby blive for varm | SIDS risk |
| Smoke around baby | Ryg omkring baby | SIDS risk |
| Sleep with baby on sofa/chair | Sov med baby på sofa/stol | High suffocation risk |

## Safe Sleep Products

### Safe

| Product | Danish | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Firm crib mattress | Fast madras | Meets safety standards |
| Fitted sheet | Lagen med elastik | Only bedding needed |
| Sleep sack/wearable blanket | Sovepose | Safe alternative to blankets |
| Pacifier | Sut | Protective factor |

### Not Recommended

| Product | Danish | Concern |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Crib bumpers | Sengehest puder | Suffocation, entrapment |
| Sleep positioners | Sovepuder | Not proven safe |
| Wedges | Kiler | Not proven safe |
| Loose blankets | Løse tæpper | Suffocation risk |
| Soft toys in crib | Bløde dyr i sengen | Suffocation risk |

## When Baby Can Roll

Once babies can roll from back to stomach AND stomach to back independently:

1. Continue placing them on their back to start
2. If they roll during sleep, you don't need to reposition
3. Ensure sleep area is safe (no soft objects)

> **Danish:** "Når barnet selv kan vende sig, behøver du ikke lægge det tilbage på ryggen."
>
> **English:** "When the baby can turn over on their own, you don't need to put them back on their back."

---

## Swaddling Safety (Svøbning)

If you choose to swaddle:

| Guideline | Danish |
|-----------|--------|
| Stop when baby shows signs of rolling | Stop når baby viser tegn på at ville rulle |
| Keep hips loose | Hold hofterne løse |
| Don't swaddle too tightly | Svøb ikke for stramt |
| Don't cover head | Dæk ikke hovedet |
| Use thin, breathable fabric | Brug tynd, åndbar stof |

**Stop swaddling by 2 months or when baby starts trying to roll.**

---

## Tummy Time (Mavetid)

While babies should sleep on their backs, supervised tummy time while awake is important:

| Benefit | Danish |
|---------|--------|
| Strengthens neck muscles | Styrker nakkemusklerne |
| Prevents flat head | Forebygger fladt hoved |
| Promotes motor development | Fremmer motorisk udvikling |

> **Danish:** "Mavetid i vågen tilstand er vigtig for babyens udvikling."
>
> **English:** "Tummy time while awake is important for baby's development."

## Key Messages for Parents

1. **Back to sleep, every sleep** - Always place baby on their back
2. **Own space, same room** - Baby in own bed, parents' room for 6 months
3. **Firm and empty** - Firm mattress, no soft objects
4. **Not too hot** - Check baby's neck, use sleep sack
5. **Smoke-free** - No smoking during pregnancy or around baby
6. **Breastfeed if possible** - Protective factor
7. **Offer a pacifier** - Protective factor at sleep time
