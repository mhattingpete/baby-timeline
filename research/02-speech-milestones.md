## Important Context

> **Danish:** "De første 24 måneder er vigtige for barnets sprogudvikling. Børn lærer sprog, når de er sammen med vigtige personer i deres liv."
>
> **English:** "The first 24 months are important for the child's language development. Children learn language when they are with important people in their lives."
>
> *Source: sundhed.dk - Tale- og sprogudvikling*

## When to Seek Guidance

Contact your sundhedsplejerske or læge if you notice:
- No babbling by 9 months
- No response to name by 12 months
- No single words by 16 months
- No two-word combinations by 24 months
- Loss of previously acquired language skills
